When a character rolls [[Risk Dice]], the result may be a Minor or Major complication. These are generally determined freeform by the GM, but either the GM or player can choose to roll for a complication on these tables.

#### Might actions

| d10 | Minor Complication                                                                                                     | Major Complication                                                                                                                                     |
| --- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 1   | Minor damage to weapon or [[Pain]] 1                                                                                   | Major damage to weapon, possibility that fragile gear is damaged or broken (flasks shatter, clothes torn, etc), possibility of [[Injury]] 1 to a limb. |
| 2   | Character shifted up to 2 meters from intended position                                                                | Character shifted up to 6 meters from intended position, prone.                                                                                        |
| 3   | Drop held item, grip slips during action                                                                               | Drop an item, including possibly worn gear                                                                                                             |
| 4   | [[Pain]] 1                                                                                                             | [[Pain]] 2                                                                                                                                             |
| 5   | [[Winded]] 1                                                                                                           | [[Winded]] 2                                                                                                                                           |
| 6   | Incidental damage to environment, area around character becomes difficult terrain                                      | Major inconvenient damage to environment - difficult terrain, collapses, stuff on fire, etc                                                            |
| 7   | Lose 1 Stamina                                                                                                         | Possibility of [[Injury]] 1 to a limb.                                                                                                                 |
| 8   | [[Disoriented]] 1                                                                                                      | [[Disoriented]] 2                                                                                                                                      |
| 9   | [[Off-balance]] 1                                                                                                      | [[Off-balance]] 2                                                                                                                                      |
| 10  | Uncontrolled damage to target or possessions or nearby extra target (slips and applies their strength to someone else) | Severe uncontrolled damage to target or possessions or nearby extra target.                                                                            |

| 2   | Nerves - character reacts to the wrong thing some time in the near future, inappropriate reflexes. Mostly cosmetic/funny. | Serious nerves - character reacts to the wrong thing some time in the future, inappropriate reflexes. Could be serious - counterattacking an ally, etc.                                                                                   |
| 3   | Drop initiative by 2 next round                                                                                           | Drop initiative by 6 next round                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| 4   | Character loses remaining movement this round / during travel interval, character slowed by 50%                           | Character loses remaining movement this round and the next / during travel interval, character makes no progress                                                                                                                          |

| 6   | Character jumps, possibly falls, hits head ([[Pain]] 1), etc.                                                             | Character jumps, more serious injuries.
